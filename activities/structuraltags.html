Structural Tags

Engagement Activity

What goes in the header?

The header usually contains the name of the website that you are creating or are in. Navigation buttons may also be inside the header of the webpage.

What goes in the Nav?

The Nav portion of the HTML code contains the navigation buttons to move around the different pages of the website. It can go before, after, or in the header. But not exclusively in the header. It is recommended that the Nav portion does not send visitors of the page or site to a different website. We want visitors to stay on our website.

What goes in Main?

The Main contains the main information, buttons, or content of the webpage.

What goes in the Footer?

The Footer of the webpage usually contains other links that may be of help to those visiting the website. It can also contain the legal entities that are required by the law to be included in certain websites and web pages.
