Marvel Cinematic Universe

The Marvel Cinematic Universe, also known as the MCU, is the universe that connects the stories, narrative, and movies of the characters in the Marvel superhero films. This table shows the top movies based on popularity and box office revenue worldwide of different movies within the franchise. The MCU is known for its stand alone superhero movies as well as cross-overs where superheroes team together to fight a common enemy.

One of the biggest movie(s) of the MCU is/are the Avengers franchise. The film displays the major Marvel Universe heroes such as Iron Man, The Hulk, Captain America, Thor, Spider-Man, and others team up to defeat extraterrestrial enemies trying to invade the planet Earth. One major nemesis of the Avengers is Thanos who appears in Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: End Game.
