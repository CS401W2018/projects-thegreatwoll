Marvel's Avengers - Spider-Man

Avengers    Spider-Man

After getting bitten by an experimental hybrid spider with DNA combined of different types, Peter Parker experienced a mutation in his own DNA where he got the characteristics and abilities of a spider. He later on became your friendly neighborhood Spider-Man. Fighting off crime, keeping peace, and fulfilling his responsibilities became his purpose in life.

Spider-Man became my favorite superhero growing up. From watching Spider-Man 1 when I was around 4 years old, he became the standard of what I imagined a great superhero would be. Now, being part of the Avengers, a group consisting of my other favorite superheros, sure blew my mind.

The motto he lives by:

"With great power, comes great responsibility."

The following are the antagonists to Spider-Man from the original franchise:

Green Goblin
A scientist turned mad villain named Norman Osborn.
The Sandman
A mutant man that can turn into sand and destroy anything that comes on its path.
Dr. Octopus
A mad scientist named Dr. Otto Octavius, who got possesed by the robotic tentacles he created that started gaining conscience on its own.

Note: Multiple other villains have been known in other films.
