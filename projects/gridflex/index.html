About the Gallery

These photos highlight some of the photographer's work as a sports photographer for the Brigham Young University - Hawaii sports department, Seasider Sports. Most of these photos were taken during the university's intramural games held last Fall Semester 2024.

The games ranged from soccer, football, basketball, and volleyball. Events were held mostly at night within the campus grounds and sports facilities.
