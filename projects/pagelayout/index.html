Welcome to My Portfolio

My name is Errol Jon Rosaroso, also known as EJ. I am a Filipino by birth and was raised in the Philippines. Now, I moved to Oahu, Hawaii to pursue a college education. I am a Computer Science Major currently studying at Brigham Young University—Hawaii. I minor in Film and Business Marketing and is expected to graduate by Spring 2028. I currently work as a sports photographer for BYU-Hawaii's sports department, Seasider Sports.

This website is a collection of my best works working as a creative for both video production and photography. My goal is to build a consistent, unique, and established creative workflow that will benefit multiple organizations when it comes to their creative needs. I have had about 3 years of experience working as a video editor and did a little bit of work on graphic design. I am a hard working and creative individual with a keen eye to details and precision.
